HOW TO READ THIS
  • Components are still grouped by what they do.
  • Each component starts with a FUNCTIONAL title (never the original directory name) and a
    "DESIGNED TO" line saying what it was built to do and roughly how.
  • Then EVERY file in that component is named, one per line, each ending in either:
        STANDOUT: <the one specific thing this file does that makes it stand out>
        N/A      (it competently fills a position, but nothing about it is irreplaceable)
  • The bar for STANDOUT is the "special cashier" bar: doing the obvious job of your category
    (a lexer that lexes, a from-scratch AES, an ELF parser, a header, a unit test, a CLI tool,
    a copied SHA-256) is N/A. STANDOUT requires a specific, niche, or irreplaceable distinction.
  • Redundancy rule: where a C reference was superseded by a live .iii module, the .iii is listed
    (the kept twin) and the C copy is omitted. The planetary layer is dropped (subsumed by federation).

A note you should weigh: per R1-SUBSYSTEMS.md, NONE of these 32 C subsystems are in the live build
chain — they are reference implementations. That is exactly why most files below are N/A: filling a
spec position is not the same as being uniquely valuable. The STANDOUT files are the few that would
be genuinely hard to replace or re-derive.

[C1]  Six-Pillar Operation-Safety Algebra with a Bricking-Impossibility Proof
      DESIGNED TO:  classify every operation as six asymmetric-ternary trits (inverse-derivability,
      causality-depth, consent-recency, replication-tier, adversariality, coherence-impact) packed
      in a u16, compose them (AND on the four structural pillars, OR on the two informational), and
      admit only hexads with no NEG in the structural pillars — so six firmware-bricking operations
      are unreachable by construction.
      FILES:
        HEXAD\src\hexad_algebra.c   STANDOUT: the math that makes the safety work. Each operation gets six
                                    "trits" (three-valued digits) and they're combined with deliberately
                                    ASYMMETRIC tables — NEG is weighted -2 so that once any structural pillar
                                    goes NEG it dominates and stays NEG ("sticky") no matter what you combine

      DESIGNED TO:  collapse an entire computing stack into ONE atom and ONE economy. The radical idea:

      type, its energy cost, and the witness of its own execution — so there is no separate compiler IR,

      FILES:

                                      execution. Nearly every other system keeps those as separate things

                                      identical 32 bytes — with a "three persons" encoding for logical intent /
                                      execution / silicon embodiment — is the single most distinctive idea here.

        III\runtime\proof_economy.h       STANDOUT: a six-tier proof system that automatically decides HOW HARD

[η1]  The Crystal / Ripple Lineage & Sealed-Evolution Engine   (the novel core inside omnia)
      DESIGNED TO:  make errors and derived values unforgeable and fully traced, propagate a

        STDLIB\iii\omnia\crystal.iii     STANDOUT: a "crystal" is a forge-resistant value — an error or derived

                                    over those fields and a sealed sub-key. Anyone who fabricates a fake error or
                                    result fails crystal_verify because they lack the sub-key. Errors that cannot be
                                    forged and always know exactly where, why, and under what authority they arose.
        STDLIB\iii\omnia\ripple.iii      STANDOUT: the change-propagation engine ("Mobius coherence"). Given one
                                    source-of-truth change it computes the transitive closure of every dependent,
                                    re-seals each one, appends a witness for every step, verifies that all those
                                    witnesses check and the K-chain stays healthy, and then ATOMICALLY commits a new
                                    closure root — returning a delta crystal. A change is therefore a witnessed,
                                    verified, all-or-nothing transaction across the whole dependency graph, not a
                                    scatter of edits.

                                    sanctioned way the system's surface may change. A proposal advances PENDING ->
